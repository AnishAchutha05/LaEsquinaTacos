About Us

🌮 Welcome to La Esquina 🌮

We are a passionate new startup—a Mexican food joint on a flavorful mission! Inspired by the vibrant beauty of Mexican folk art, we bring not just food, but the soul of Mexico right to the streets of India.

At La Esquina, we offer a wide and exquisite assortment of authentic Mexican dishes, crafted to delight your taste buds and introduce you to the rich, bold flavors of New Mexico. This is more than just a meal—it’s a cultural experience.
